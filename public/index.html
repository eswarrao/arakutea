The Craft

Ancient Wisdom.
Precise Craft.

I
Plucking
Only the finest two leaves and a bud are hand-selected at dawn, when the plant’s essential oils reach their peak concentration.
II
Withering
Freshly plucked leaves rest in cool mountain air for 18 hours, slowly releasing moisture and concentrating the leaf’s innate flavour profile.
III
Rolling
Gentle pressure breaks cell walls, releasing enzymes that begin the alchemical transformation into a multi-layered, complex brew.
IV
Firing
Precision heat at 95°C halts oxidation at the perfect moment, locking in the signature character of each single-estate batch.
